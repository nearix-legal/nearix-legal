Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This policy explains how Nearix collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

01Information we collect

  • Google account data: your name and email address via Google Sign-In.
  • Profile data: photos, age, gender, country, preferred language, bio, interests, and an optional voice introduction.
  • Chat content: text, image, and voice messages exchanged between users.
  • Usage data: last-active timestamp (for online status), and in-app activity such as profile visits and likes.
  • Advertising data: your device's advertising identifier and basic technical data collected by Google AdMob to serve ads.

02How we use your data

  • To create and display your profile to other users in Discover.
  • To enable direct messaging between users.
  • To show online status, badges (like "New"), and shared-interest compatibility.
  • To review reports and act on accounts that violate our terms.
  • To display ads — the app's sole source of funding, and it will always remain free.

03Third-party sharing

We rely on a small number of trusted providers and never sell your data:

  • Google Firebase: authentication and database storage for profiles and chats.
  • Cloudinary: hosting for photos and voice messages you upload.
  • Google AdMob: ad serving; may collect device-level technical data under its own policy.

04Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account — this immediately and permanently removes your profile, conversations, and all associated data.

05Data security

Your data travels over encrypted connections, and strict database access rules ensure no user can access another user's private data beyond what the app needs to function.

06Children's privacy

This app is strictly for people 18 and older. We require age confirmation at signup and do not knowingly collect data from minors. Any account found to belong to a minor is deleted immediately.

07Your rights

  • Access the data stored about you at any time.
  • Edit your profile data directly in the app.
  • Permanently delete your account and all your data in one tap.
  • Contact us with any privacy question.

08Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any change will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above.
